JEMFEST 2017

TUESDAY, MAY 9TH

ALAMO DRAFTHOUSE, THEATER 7

doors open at 6pm | showtime at 6:45pm

 

JEMFest is an annual showcase and celebration of excellent audio and video production created by students in Journalism and Electronic Media.

Thank you for your interest in showcasing your AWESOME stuff! We look forward to seeing it, but first, let’s make sure it adheres to some guidelines. Scroll down or choose a section below to see the guidelines.

You may submit multiple types of media. Please submit to Professor Erica Taylor’s office in CoMC 405. SUBMISSIONS ARE DUE TUESDAY, MARCH 21 BY 5 P.M.
Submission Form

NOTE: Submissions may be rejected for content – although there are no restrictions on content, all items will be reviewed for their value and quality.

  • Video
  • Audio

Video

  • Video should be formatted to 1080p or 720p MP4 file.
  • Videos may not exceed 8 minutes.
  • Videos must be submitted in HARD COPY (Flash Drive, Hard Drive, or SD card) with a submission form.

Audio

  • Audio must be exported in an MP3 format.
  • Audio may not exceed 8 minutes.
  • Audio submissions must be submitted in HARD COPY (Flash Drive, Hard Drive, or SD card) with a submission form.
