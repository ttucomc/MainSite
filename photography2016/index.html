Showcase and Competition

Show off your photography, create community, and win photography gear!

Thanks for your interest in submitting your still photography to the first PHOTOGRAPH: Showcase and Competition. Image submissions for this year’s competition will take place up through November 9, and 30 selected images and winners will be exhibited in print at the official PHOTOGRAPH event on December 1. Category winners and Best of Show will be announced that the event as well!

Digital submission only. Images must be submitted via email as JPEGs no larger than 2,500 pixels on the long edge.

Submission deadline is Wednesday, November 9 at midnight.

Email all images to photos.comc@ttu.edu with the linked submission form below (print/fill out and reattach to email).
Submission Form

Landscape/Nature

  • Images that showcase the natural environment.
  • Examples include traditional landscape, macro photography, and wildlife.

Portraiture

  • Images that focus on the human subject.
  • Examples include traditional and commercial portraiture, environmental portraits, and lifestyle.

Editorial

  • Images made in an effort to convey a part of a narrative.
  • Examples include photojournalism, magazine photography, and any images made on assignment.
  • Editorial images must include a caption per image not to exceed 250 words.

Sports/Action

  • Images emphasizing athleticism, adventure, and/or recreation in motion.
  • Examples include sports game action, non-traditional sports, and outdoor adventure/recreation activities.
  • Sports portraiture should be submitted to the Portraiture category.

Miscellaneous

  • Images that focus on areas not necessarily outlined in previous categories.
  • Examples might include abstract or digital art created around the photograph as a foundation and performed in Adobe Photoshop.

You may submit up to five images total, including multiple images to one category. Please submit images by email to photos.comc@ttu.edu by midnight, November 9. Include the linked form to provide submission details to contest and showcase administration (print and re-attach to email).
